## 附录: 核心方程

### A. 期望自由能
$$G(\pi) = \underbrace{-\mathbb{E}_{Q}[\ln P(o|\pi)]}_{\text{实用价值}} - \underbrace{\mathbb{E}_{Q}[H[P(o|s)]]}_{\text{信息价值}} + \underbrace{\gamma \cdot \text{Recip}(\pi)}_{\text{互惠价值}}$$

### B. 贝叶斯模型选择
$$\Delta F = F(M_1) - F(M_0) = -\ln \frac{P(D|M_1)}{P(D|M_0)} + \lambda \cdot \Delta K$$

### C. 内化更新
$$\gamma_{t+1} = \begin{cases} \gamma_t \times 1.05 & \text{if reciprocated} \\ \gamma_t / 1.05 & \text{if betrayed} \end{cases}$$

### D. 信号强度（先验效应）
$$\text{Signal} = \frac{\delta}{E[p]} = \frac{\text{bias}}{\alpha / (\alpha + \beta)}$$
